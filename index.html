What we do

Built for the way
India's clinics work.

Not a foreign product retrofitted for India. Built from the ground up — for Indian languages, Indian patients, and the way Indian doctors actually operate.

“Your receptionist handles 30 calls a day. Your AI handles 300 — simultaneously, in 5 languages, at 3 in the morning.”
01 — Voice

Speaks your patients' language

Patients call your existing clinic number and speak naturally in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, or English. No IVR menus. Just a conversation that ends with a confirmed appointment.

Voice AI
02 — WhatsApp

Doctors manage via WhatsApp

No app to install. Doctors receive booking notifications, view daily schedules, mark leave, and cancel slots — all through WhatsApp. The tool they already use every day.

WhatsApp
03 — Booking

Token queues and time slots — both

High-demand specialists get a token queue with atomic assignment — no double bookings even with simultaneous callers. Time-based clinics get Google Calendar integration.

Smart Booking
04 — Follow-ups

Patients followed up automatically

Doctors send follow-up instructions at end of day via WhatsApp. The system calls patients next morning, reports their status back to the doctor before noon.

Care Continuity
05 — Staff

Receptionist app on any phone

Open a web link on any phone. See today's queue. Tap Attended or No-show per patient. Real-time queue display. Replaces the paper register your receptionist still uses.

Attendance
06 — Analytics

Dashboard that answers one question

Are you growing? Monthly patients, no-show rates, busiest days, most common issues, doctor performance — all in a clean dashboard your staff can open on any phone.

  • One receptionist answering calls, managing queue, taking payments
